Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 1, 2025  ·  Last updated: June 1, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to PouchFit ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use the PouchFit mobile application ("App").

By using PouchFit, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you disagree with any part, please discontinue use of the App.

2. Information We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to operate and improve PouchFit. This includes:

  • Usage data — Exercise sessions completed, duration, streaks, daily goals, and which apps you've configured to be blocked. Stored locally on your device and optionally in iCloud if sync is enabled.
  • Onboarding preferences — Your weekly movement goal and routine preferences. Stored locally.
  • Crash and diagnostic data — Anonymous error reports to help us fix bugs. Contains no personally identifiable information.

We do not collect your name, email address, location, health records, or any information that could identify you personally, unless you voluntarily contact us for support.

3. How We Use Your Information

Information collected is used exclusively to:

  • Display your personal progress, streaks, and goal tracking
  • Manage your app-blocking schedule using Apple's Screen Time framework
  • Enable Live Activity widgets showing your active session on the iOS lock screen
  • Identify and fix technical errors through anonymous crash logs

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any commercial purpose beyond operating the App.

4. Data Storage and Security

All personal fitness data is stored locally on your device using Apple's standard iOS data protection mechanisms. App-blocking is handled through Apple's Screen Time API — PouchFit never sees the contents of the apps you choose to block.

We take reasonable precautions to protect your data, but no method of storage is 100% secure. We encourage you to use a device passcode and keep your iOS version up to date.

5. Third-Party Services

PouchFit integrates with the following Apple platform services:

  • Apple Screen Time / FamilyControls framework — Powers the app-blocking feature. Data is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy and never leaves your device.
  • Apple ActivityKit — Powers Live Activity lock screen widgets. Governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.
  • iCloud (optional) — If enabled, app data may sync across your devices. Governed by Apple's iCloud Terms.

We do not integrate any third-party advertising SDKs, analytics platforms, or social login systems. PouchFit is completely ad-free.

6. Children's Privacy

PouchFit is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal data from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

7. Your Rights

Because PouchFit stores data locally on your device, you have full control:

  • Reset all your data at any time from the App's Settings screen
  • Deleting the App removes all locally stored data immediately
  • Disable iCloud backup for PouchFit in iOS Settings → [Your Name] → iCloud

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have rights under GDPR including access, rectification, erasure, and objection. Contact us to exercise these rights.

8. Data Retention

We do not retain personal data on our servers. All fitness and goal data lives on your device (and optionally iCloud). Anonymous crash logs are retained for up to 90 days and then automatically deleted.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Your continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact Us
