Movement Disclaimer & Health Notice

Effective date: May 31, 2026 · Last updated: May 31, 2026

PouchFit is a micro-movement app designed to encourage light physical activity throughout your day. The exercise routines, guidance, and content provided within this app (the "Content") are intended for general wellness purposes only.

Not Medical Advice

The Content is not medical advice and should not be treated as such. PouchFit is not a licensed medical provider, physiotherapist, or health professional. Nothing in this app creates a patient-provider relationship or constitutes a diagnosis, treatment plan, or prescription of any kind.

Always consult a qualified healthcare professional before beginning any new physical activity, particularly if you have a pre-existing medical condition, injury, chronic illness, are pregnant, postpartum, or have been recently discharged from medical care.

Not a Substitute for Exercise

PouchFit is designed as a supplement to — not a replacement for — your regular physical activity, fitness routine, or any exercise program recommended by a healthcare or fitness professional.

The routines in this app consist of short-duration micro-movements intended to reduce prolonged sedentary behavior. They do not constitute a complete fitness or rehabilitation program.

Exercise at Your Own Pace

All routines are guides, not commands. Every body is different. If any movement causes pain, discomfort, dizziness, shortness of breath, or any unusual sensation, stop immediately and do not attempt to push through it.

Modify freely, skip what doesn't feel right, and always prioritize your safety and comfort over completing a session. No routine is worth an injury.

Assumption of Risk

By using PouchFit, you acknowledge that physical activity carries inherent risk, and you voluntarily assume full responsibility for any injury, discomfort, or adverse outcome that may result from following the routines in this app.

PouchFit and its developers are not liable for any harm arising from use or misuse of the Content.

When in Doubt, Stop

If you are unsure whether an exercise is appropriate for you, skip it.

If you experience chest pain, sudden dizziness, severe joint pain, or any symptom that concerns you, stop all activity and seek medical attention immediately.

PouchFit is here to help you move a little more — not to replace the professionals who know your body best.
